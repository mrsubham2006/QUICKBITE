Frequently Asked Questions

Order & Delivery

How do I track my order?

Go to My Orders section in your profile. You can see real-time updates for all active orders.

What is the estimated delivery time?

Delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location and restaurant preparation time.

Payments & Refunds

How can I get a refund?

Refunds are initiated automatically for cancelled orders. It usually takes 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.

What are the payment methods accepted?

We accept all major Credit/Debit cards, UPI ID, and QuickBite Wallet.

Dineout & Reservations

How to cancel a table reservation?

You can cancel reservations up to 2 hours before the scheduled time from the My Bookings section.
